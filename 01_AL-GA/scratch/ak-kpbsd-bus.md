1.18. Routing Terms
1.18.1. Run The shortest distance necessary to transport a given group of students from scheduled pick-up points to an attendance center or from an attendance center to scheduled discharge points. Bus runs also include distances between pick-up points and transfer points from one bus run to another when transporting students to and from attendance centers, and when more than one attendance center is served on the same bus run. A run does not include the distance from where the bus is garaged to the beginning of the run or from the end of the run to where the bus is garaged.
1.18.2. Segment One or more runs transporting students to or from an attendance center within a certain time frame. A morning segment includes the distances from the first attendance center drop-off to the first pick-up point on the ensuing run(s), if any. An afternoon segment includes the distances from the final drop-off point of the first run to the ensuing attendance center loading zone(s), if any. Midday segments can include to-attendance center runs and from-attendance center runs. A segment does not include the distance from where the bus is garaged to the beginning of the run or from the end of the run to where the bus is garaged.
1.18.2.1. Live Miles The bus run mileage between the first student pick-up point and the final student discharge point as established by the District. Live mileage, as applied to the a.m. shall be calculated by starting at the first student pickup point farthest from for the first school to be served, to that school, and accumulating as the bus proceeds to the first pickup point for the second run, to that school, and accumulating as the bus proceeds to the first pickup point for the third run and delivering those students to school. Live miles for the a.m. run shall end when the bus has discharged pupils at the last attendance center to be served for the morning segment. Live miles for the
a.m. do not include miles from where the bus is garaged to the first pick-up point or miles from the school drop to the where the bus is garaged. Live mileage, as applied to the p.m., shall be calculated by starting at the first school to be served, accumulating as the bus discharges students and proceeds to the second attendance center to be served, accumulating as the bus discharges students and proceeds to the third attendance center, and accumulating as these students are discharged. Live mileage for the p.m. shall end when the bus delivers to the last discharge point from the last attendance center served for the afternoon segment. Live miles for the p.m. do not include miles from the last discharge point to where the bus is garaged. Live mileage for midday segments shall be calculated using the same methods outlined above.

## Total miles minus live miles.
